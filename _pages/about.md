I am a Ph.D. student at the Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University, advised in the area of computer application technology. I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Wuhan University (GPA 3.88/4.0, top 1% for two academic years). My research interests include **3D/4D generation**, **video generation**, **motion generation**, **digital humans**, and **world models**.

I am currently an algorithm intern at **Kuaishou Kling AI**, working on general object motion transfer, camera motion cloning, and related video generation problems. My recent work spans cross-species motion transfer, 3D point cloud generation, and out-of-distribution generalization for 3D perception.

I am actively seeking research and algorithm positions in generative AI, multimodal learning, video generation, world models, and embodied AI.
