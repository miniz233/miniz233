**OmniDirector** targets general multi-shot camera cloning for video generation without cross-paired training data.

During my internship at Kuaishou Kling AI, I contributed to reproducing baseline methods, validating camera motion cloning results, and supporting experimental analysis for this project.

The work explores controllable camera motion transfer and cloning in complex video generation scenarios.
